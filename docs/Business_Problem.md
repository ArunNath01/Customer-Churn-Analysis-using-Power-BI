# Business Problem

## Background

Customer retention is a critical factor in the telecommunications industry. Acquiring new customers is generally more expensive than retaining existing ones, making customer churn a significant business concern.

The company has observed that a portion of its customers are discontinuing their services. Management requires an analytical dashboard that provides visibility into customer churn patterns, identifies high-risk customer segments, and measures the financial impact of customer attrition.

## Business Objective

Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables decision-makers to:

* Monitor customer churn performance.
* Measure the overall churn rate.
* Identify customer segments with high churn.
* Analyze how contract type, tenure, and internet service influence churn.
* Evaluate the revenue lost due to customer attrition.
* Support data-driven customer retention strategies.

## Success Criteria

The dashboard should enable stakeholders to answer the following business questions:

1. What is the current customer churn rate?
2. Which customer groups have the highest churn?
3. Which contract types are associated with increased churn?
4. How does customer tenure affect churn?
5. Which internet service category experiences the highest customer loss?
6. What is the financial impact of customer churn?
7. Which customers should be prioritized for retention initiatives?

## Expected Business Value

The completed dashboard will provide management with a centralized view of customer retention performance and support strategic decisions aimed at reducing churn and protecting revenue.
