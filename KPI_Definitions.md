# KPI Definitions

## Total Customers

**Definition:** Total number of customers in the dataset.

**Business Purpose:** Represents the customer base used for analysis.

---

## Churned Customers

**Definition:** Number of customers whose churn status is "Yes".

**Business Purpose:** Measures the total number of customers lost.

---

## Churn Rate

**Formula**

Churn Rate = Churned Customers ÷ Total Customers

**Business Purpose**

Measures the percentage of customers who discontinued the service.

---

## Total Revenue

**Definition**

Sum of Total Charges for all customers.

**Business Purpose**

Represents the cumulative revenue generated by the customer base.

---

## Revenue Lost

**Definition**

Sum of Total Charges for customers who churned.

**Business Purpose**

Measures the financial impact of customer attrition.

---

## Average Monthly Charges

**Definition**

Average monthly subscription charge across all customers.

**Business Purpose**

Helps determine whether pricing influences churn behavior.

---

## Average Customer Revenue

**Definition**

Average Total Charges per customer.

**Business Purpose**

Provides insight into the average customer value.
